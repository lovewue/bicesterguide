The Bicester Guide is an independent visitor guide designed to help people make the most of their time in and around Bicester Village.

Why we created the guide

Every year, millions of people visit Bicester Village, but many never discover the hotels, restaurants, cafés, historic houses, countryside attractions, independent businesses and local experiences nearby.

The Bicester Guide brings this information together in one place, helping visitors plan a day trip, weekend break or longer stay with confidence.

What you’ll find here

You’ll find guides to places to stay, eat, shop and explore, along with practical visitor information, transport tips, local services and hidden gems around Bicester and beyond.

How the site is funded

Some links on The Bicester Guide may be affiliate links. If you make a booking or purchase through one of these links, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.
