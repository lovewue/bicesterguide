Getting there

Bicester Village is easy to reach by both train and car, with direct rail links from London and convenient road access from the M40.

By train

Direct Chiltern Railways services run from London Marylebone to Bicester Village (towards Oxford) and Bicester North (towards Birmingham), making rail one of the easiest ways to visit. If stopping at Bicester North a short bus ride is required to reach the village.

By car

Bicester Village is close to Junction 9 of the M40 and is straightforward to reach by road, though weekends and peak sale periods can be busier.

Top Tip: After you leave the M40 make sure you stick to the speed limit as there is often a speed trap on this road, especially when Bicester Village is busy
