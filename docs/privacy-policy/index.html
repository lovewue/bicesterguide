Introduction

The Bicester Guide ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

The Bicester Guide is an independent visitor information website providing guides, recommendations and local information about Bicester, Bicester Village and the surrounding area.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights relating to your personal information, including access, correction, deletion and withdrawal of consent where applicable.
